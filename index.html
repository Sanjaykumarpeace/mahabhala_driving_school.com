Who We Are

Bengaluru's Trusted
Driving School

Mahabhala Driving School has been empowering drivers across North Bengaluru for over a decade. Located at JM Complex, Kattigenahalli, opposite REVA College Main Road, we are conveniently accessible from Yelahanka, Jakkur, Hebbal, and surrounding areas.

Our RTO-registered instructors combine patience with proven teaching methodologies — from mastering basics to navigating Bengaluru's dynamic traffic with confidence.
